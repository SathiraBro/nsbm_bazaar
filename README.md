🛍️ NSBM Student Marketplace

A modern web-based e-commerce platform developed as a university project to help small businesses sell products to NSBM students and staff.

⸻

📖 Overview

The NSBM Student Marketplace is a web application that provides an online shopping experience for university students and staff. Customers can register, browse products, search for items, place orders, and complete payments through a simple and user-friendly interface.

The platform also includes an Admin Dashboard where administrators can manage products and monitor customer orders.
